FAQ

Questions, answered.

Still curious? Email hello@buddysaradhi.in — a human replies in 24h.

How many students can I add on the free plan?
Unlimited. Whether you teach 12 students or 250, every feature works the same. We don't believe in charging per-seat for software that should just work.
What happens to my data if Buddysaradhi shuts down?
Your data is yours. Export to a single encrypted SQLite file at any time. If we ever sunset, you'll get 90 days notice and a one-click full export — including receipts, ledger entries, and audit log.
Does attendance work without internet?
Yes. The mobile and desktop apps queue every action locally and sync the moment you're back online. Conflict resolution uses last-write-wins on attendance and a hard reject on conflicting fee entries.
Are receipts legally valid for parents?
Every receipt carries a sequential number, a timestamp, a tutor signature line, and a SHA-256 hash that chains to the previous receipt. Parents can verify any receipt against your ledger at any time.
Can I use Buddysaradhi for multiple subjects / batches?
Yes — create as many batches as you like. Each batch has its own roster, attendance schedule, and fee calendar. Switch batches in one tap from the sidebar.
Will it stay free forever?
Free while our infra stays free. When that math changes — if it ever does — you'll know 90 days in advance. Existing tutors keep their plan; only new sign-ups migrate. No surprise bills.
